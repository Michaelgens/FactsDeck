## Act I — The opening chord

Your first money decision of the month sets the tone—like the first color you lay on a blank page. Start with clarity, not intensity.

![Abstract gradient suggesting dawn over a calm financial horizon — purple and amber tones](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1611974789855-9c2a0a7236a3?w=1200&h=600&fit=crop&q=80)

*Caption: Markets move in cycles; your plan should move in **intentional** strokes.*

### Three beats to hit this week

1. **Snapshot** — One screen: cash, debt, investments. No judgment, just data.
2. **One lever** — Raise a 401(k) percent, kill a subscription, or add $50 to savings. Pick *one*.
3. **Date it** — Put the next review on your calendar. Rhythm beats heroics.
